Website Evaluation

Good Website: Microsoft

Microsoft's website is known for its professional design, clear navigation, and accessibility features.

  • Contrast: Uses a balanced color scheme with strong readability
  • Repetition: Consistent typography, branding, and layout across pages
  • Alignment: Well-structured elements provide a clean and professional appearance
  • Proximity: Content is logically grouped, making it easy to find relevant information

Bad Website: Arngren

Arngren's website is an example of outdated and cluttered design with poor usability.

  • Contrast: Excessive use of bright colors and overlapping elements makes text difficult to read
  • Repetition: Lacks consistency in fonts, images, and layout, creating visual confusion
  • Alignment: Elements are scattered haphazardly, making it visually unappealing
  • Proximity: Overcrowded content with little spacing makes navigation frustrating
